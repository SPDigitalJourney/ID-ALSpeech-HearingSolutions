What is Cochlear Implant Mapping?

Precise Programming for
Maximum Sound Clarity

Cochlear implant mapping optimises the performance of a cochlear implant by precisely programming the speech processor to meet each individual's hearing needs. It helps CI recipients achieve the best possible access to sound and speech understanding.

Without regular mapping, a cochlear implant cannot deliver optimal performance. The needs of a CI recipient change over time — especially in the months after activation — making ongoing mapping sessions critical.
