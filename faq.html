Diagnostic Tests

OAE, ABR & VEMP Tests

OAE (Otoacoustic Emissions) is a completely painless, non-invasive test. A small probe is placed in the ear canal and measures sounds produced by the inner ear (cochlea). It takes only 2–5 minutes and is commonly used for newborn screening. There is no discomfort whatsoever.
An ABR (Auditory Brainstem Response) test typically takes 45 minutes to 1.5 hours depending on complexity. For infants, a sleep-state ABR may be required, which means the child should be naturally sleepy or mildly sedated. Our audiologists will guide you on preparation before your appointment.
VEMP is recommended for anyone experiencing unexplained dizziness, balance problems, or vertigo. It is also used to diagnose Ménière's disease, superior canal dehiscence syndrome (SCDS), and vestibular neuritis. Your ENT or audiologist will recommend VEMP if clinically indicated.
Early detection is critical. Hearing loss that is not identified before 6 months of age can significantly delay speech and language development. Universal Newborn Hearing Screening using OAE/ABR is a standard recommended protocol. IDÉAL provides this service gently and safely.

Cochlear™ Mapping

Cochlear Implant Questions

Cochlear implant mapping (also called "programming" or "tuning") is the process of customising the settings of a cochlear implant speech processor to match the user's individual hearing needs. It involves setting stimulation levels for each electrode to ensure the best possible sound perception. Regular mapping is essential for optimal CI performance.
Cochlear™ Implant Mapping requires specialised equipment, software, and certified audiologist training. IDÉAL invested in this capability specifically to serve the Mangalore community — so patients no longer need to travel to Bangalore or other cities for this essential service.
Mapping frequency varies. In the first year after activation, mapping is typically done every 1–3 months. As hearing stabilises, annual or biannual sessions are common. Children may require more frequent mapping than adults. Our audiologists will create a personalised schedule for you.
