Advanced Diagnostics

State-of-the-Art Auditory Tests

Mangalore's first complete hearing care diagnostic centre — a full suite of advanced audiological tests for all ages, from newborns to adults.

Test 01

OAE — Otoacoustic Emissions

A quick, non-invasive test measuring sounds produced by the inner ear in response to a stimulus. Essential for newborn screening and detecting cochlear dysfunction before noticeable hearing loss occurs.

Test 02

Newborn Hearing Screening

Early identification of hearing loss in newborns is critical for speech and language development. Our gentle, safe programme uses OAE and ABR technology to detect hearing issues at the earliest possible stage.

Test 03

ABR — Auditory Brainstem Response

ABR measures the electrical activity of the auditory nerve and brainstem in response to sound. Gold-standard test for diagnosing hearing loss in infants, young children, and difficult-to-test patients.

Test 04

VEMP — Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potentials

VEMP tests evaluate the saccule and utricle — inner ear organs responsible for balance. Invaluable for identifying vestibular disorders, superior canal dehiscence syndrome, and related balance conditions.
