Advanced Diagnostics

State-of-the-Art Auditory Tests

As Mangalore's first complete hearing care diagnostic centre, we offer a full suite of advanced audiological tests for patients of all ages — from newborns to adults.

Test 01

OAE — Otoacoustic Emissions

A quick, non-invasive test that measures sounds produced by the inner ear (cochlea) in response to a stimulus. OAE testing is essential for newborn screening and for detecting cochlear dysfunction before it causes noticeable hearing loss.

Test 02

Newborn Hearing Screening

Early identification of hearing loss in newborns is critical for speech and language development. Our gentle, safe and reliable newborn hearing screening programme uses OAE and ABR technology to detect hearing issues at the earliest possible stage.

Test 03

ABR — Auditory Brainstem Response

ABR measures the electrical activity of the auditory nerve and brainstem pathways in response to sound. It is a gold-standard test for diagnosing hearing loss in infants, young children, and in cases where conventional audiometry is not possible.

Test 04

VEMP — Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potentials

VEMP tests evaluate the saccule and utricle — small organs in the inner ear responsible for balance. This advanced diagnostic is invaluable in identifying vestibular disorders, superior canal dehiscence syndrome, and related balance conditions.
