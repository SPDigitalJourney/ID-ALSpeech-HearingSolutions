Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and hearing care.

What is an ABR test and who needs it?+
ABR (Auditory Brainstem Response) measures electrical activity of the auditory nerve and brainstem. It is the gold-standard test for diagnosing hearing loss in infants, young children, and patients where conventional audiometry isn't possible.
What is an OAE test?+
OAE (Otoacoustic Emissions) is a quick, painless test that measures sounds produced by the inner ear. Essential for newborn hearing screening and detecting cochlear dysfunction before noticeable hearing loss occurs.
What is Cochlear Implant Mapping?+
Cochlear Implant Mapping precisely programs a cochlear implant's speech processor to match the recipient's individual hearing needs. IDÉAL is the only clinic in Mangalore providing this service for Cochlear™ recipients.
How much does a hearing aid cost in Mangalore?+
Hearing aid costs vary based on technology level and brand. At IDÉAL, we offer genuine hearing aids from Phonak, Widex, Signia, GN ReSound and Unitron across a range of price points. Contact us for a personalised recommendation.
When should a newborn undergo hearing screening?+
Every newborn should undergo hearing screening within the first month of life. Early identification is critical for speech and language development. We offer gentle, safe newborn hearing screening using OAE and ABR technology.
Do you provide home care services?+
Yes. We provide professional audiological home care services anywhere in the Mangalore district — no restrictions on location. Ideal for elderly patients, those with mobility challenges, or post-surgical follow-ups.
What is a VEMP test?+
VEMP (Vestibular Evoked Myogenic Potentials) evaluates inner ear organs responsible for balance. Used to identify vestibular disorders and related balance conditions. Available at both our clinic locations in Mangalore.
Are you an authorised hearing aid dealer?+
Yes. We are authorised dealers for Phonak, Unitron, Widex, Signia and GN ReSound. All products are genuine, manufacturer-backed, and come with full warranty and expert fitting.
